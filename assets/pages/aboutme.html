About Me

I work where technology meets visual creativity. My work includes web design, video editing, photography, and digital visuals, supported by a strong technical background.
I design and build websites, create visual concepts, and experiment with cinematic storytelling through video and photography. Alongside the creative side, I also work with servers, networks, and the technical systems behind the scenes.
I like understanding how things work — from the visuals people see to the infrastructure running underneath. This approach allows me to create projects that are both visually engaging and technically solid.
I focus on clean design, strong aesthetics, and attention to detail.
